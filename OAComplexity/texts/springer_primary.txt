<h1 id="c2188">
Publish open access with Springer
</h1>




<div class="cms-richtext"><p class="intro--paragraph">Publishing open access makes your work immediately and permanently available online for everyone, worldwide. Springer is pleased to offer a variety of open access options for our authors to publish their research.</p><p class="intro--paragraph">No matter which option you choose, all open access publications are subject to high-quality peer review, editorial and production processes. You retain copyright and can easily comply with open access mandates.</p><h4>Partner with us for your next open access book series</h4><p>We partner with funders, research centres, and organisations to publish open access books series under our SpringerOpen and Palgrave Macmillan imprints. <a href="https://www.springernature.com/gp/open-research/journals-books/books/partner-series" target="_blank" class="is-external">Read more about this opportunity here.&nbsp;</a>&nbsp;<br /><br /></p></div>
