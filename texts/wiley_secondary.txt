<h1>Publish open access with OnlineOpen</h1>

<p>Most of Wiley's subscription-based journals offer you an open access option called OnlineOpen, allowing you to publish open access in top journals.</p></br>

<h2>Benefits</h2>

<div class="table_wrapper">

<table>
    <tr>
        <td>
            <ul>
                <li><b>High visibility</b> all articles are immediately, freely available online</li>
                <li><b>Easy compliance with open access mandates</b> with Creative Commons licenses</li>
                <li><b>Reuse</b> and immediate deposit of final article in any website or repository</li>
                <li><b>Copyright retention</b> &ndash; You retain the copyright for your article at all times</li>
                <li><b>Automatic export</b> to PubMed Central/Europe PubMed Central (PMC) when appropriate*</li>

<h2>How to select OnlineOpen</h2>

<p>Once your article is accepted, the <span title="The corresponding author is the author who manages the manuscript and correspondence during the publication process - from submission through publication. This author has the authority to act on behalf of all the co-authors and will be the contact for inquiries after publication also." style="background-color:#E7FDFF;color:#597BA9;text-decoration: underline;text-decoration-style:dashed;">corresponding author</span> is able to select OnlineOpen from the <a target="_blank" href="https://authorservices.wiley.com/index.html#dashboard">Author Services Dashboard</a>. For step-by-step instructions, visit our <a href="https://authorservices.wiley.com/author-resources/Journal-Authors/open-access/how-to-order-onlineopen.html">How to Order OnlineOpen</a> page.</p><br />

<p>Once your order is complete, your article will be made open access immediately upon publication. The open access icon and Creative Commons license signals to readers that the content is freely available to read, download and share.</p><br />

<img class="amimage amcenter" src="/asset/Open-Access-Article-Notation-Wiley-Online-Library-600x277.png" alt="Open Access Article Notation | OnlineOpen | Wiley Online Library" /><br />
