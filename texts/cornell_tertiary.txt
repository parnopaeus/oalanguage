<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Criteria to consider

</h2>

<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-629504" >

<div class="s-lib-box-content">



<div id="s-lg-content-1170095" class=" clearfix">

<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium; color: #000000;"><strong>Before deciding to publish in a new OA journal, consider the following criteria:</strong></span></p>



<ul>

<li>The journal is included in the&nbsp;<a href="https://doaj.org/" target="_blank">Directory of Open Access Journals</a>. Journals indexed in&nbsp;DOAJ&nbsp;after March 2014 (noted with&nbsp;a green check mark or &quot;DOAJ&nbsp;Seal&quot;), underwent a more stringent review process than journals indexed prior&nbsp;to that date.</li>

<li><a href="http://thinkchecksubmit.org/" target="_blank">ThinkCheckSubmit</a>&nbsp;-&nbsp;This tool, produced with the support of a coalition from across scholarly communications in response to discussions about deceptive publishing,&nbsp;walks you through the process of evaluating journals.</li>

<li><span style="font-size: small;">The journal is indexed in an established and reputable database such as <a href="http://ip-science.thomsonreuters.com/mjl/#journal_lists" target="_blank">Web of Knowledge</a>, <a href="http://resolver.library.cornell.edu/misc/4135101/" target="_blank">Ulrich&#39;s</a>, or <a href="http://www.csa.com/factsheets/supplements/sociossl.php" target="_blank">Sociological Abstracts</a> (see sidebar).</span></li>

<li><span style="font-size: small;">Examine the journal&#39;s website for such information as affiliation with a university or professional organization, editorial board credentials, or acceptance rates.&nbsp;If you have doubts, contact members of the editorial board or article authors.</span></li>

<li><span style="font-size: small;">Publishing fees and copyright ownership are clearly indicated.&nbsp;For more information about your rights as an author, see the <a href="http://guides.library.cornell.edu/aecontent.php?pid=472614&amp;sid=3900603" target="_self">Know your copyrights</a>&nbsp;tab.</span></li>

<li><span style="font-size: small;">The publisher is a member of a reputable industry organization such as <a href="http://oaspa.org/membership/members/" target="_blank">Open Access Scholarly Publishers Association</a> or the <a href="http://publicationethics.org/members" target="_blank">Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE)</a>.</span></li>

</ul>



<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: x-small;">Adapted from <a href="http://libguides.govst.edu/predatorypublishing">Paul Blobaum&#39;s</a> Checklist for Review of Journal Quality, Governor&#39;s State University</span></p>

</div>

<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Ethics in OA Publishing

</h2>

<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-629505" >

<div class="s-lib-box-content">



<div id="s-lg-content-1170096" class=" clearfix">

<p>With the advent of open access publishing, printing barriers are removed and the assembly and dissemination of information requires little more than Internet access.&nbsp;Unfortunately, this has led to a proliferation of open access publishers with little or no subject expertise and of questionable repute.&nbsp;Scholars and researchers may receive email solicitations for fee-based paper submissions to journals that make false or misleading claims about the stringency of their peer-review process, members of their editorial board or indexing status. </p>



<ul>

<li>The <a href="http://publicationethics.org/" target="_blank">Committee on Publication Ethics</a> is &quot;a forum for editors and publishers of peer reviewed journals to discuss all aspects of publication ethics&quot;.</li>

</ul>



<p style="text-align: left;"><a href="http://publicationethics.org/" target="_blank"><img height="80" src="http://lgimages.s3.amazonaws.com/data/imagemanager/66248/cope.png" style="margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; display: block;" width="389" /></a></p>



<p style="text-align: left;">&nbsp;</p>



<ul>

<li>The <a href="http://oaspa.org/" target="_blank">Open Access Scholarly Publishers Association</a> is a forum for open access publishers.&nbsp;Membership is vetted and can be an indication of publisher quality.</li>

</ul>



<p><a href="http://oaspa.org/" target="_blank"><img height="49" src="http://lgimages.s3.amazonaws.com/data/imagemanager/66248/oaspa.png" style="border: 1px solid black; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto; display: block;" width="389" /></a></p>



<p>&nbsp;</p>
