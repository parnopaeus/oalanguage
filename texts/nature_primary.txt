<h1 class="small-space-below title-color strong line-below">What is open access?</h1><div><p>Open access (OA) refers to free, unrestricted online access to research outputs such as journal articles and books. OA content is open to all, with no access fees.</p>
<p>There are two main routes to making research outputs openly accessible. Find out more <a href=http://preview.springernature.com/gp/open-research/about/what-is-open-access>here</a>.</p>
