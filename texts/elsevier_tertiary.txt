<h2 id="fact1"><span class="elsorange">Fact 1:</span> Elsevier Publishes Open Access</h2><p>We are one of the leading open access publishers, with over 27,000 open access articles published in 2017 and making thousands more available through green open access.</p><p><strong>Our open access options include:</strong></p>
</div>
</div><div class="article-content"><div class="row row-tight">
    <div class="large-6 columns">
        <h3>Gold open access:</h3><p>After publication, the final article is published open access on ScienceDirect and is immediately free to access for everyone. Authors can choose to publish their article with either a commercial (CC BY) or noncommercial (CC BY NC ND) user license.</p><p>We publish:</p><ul><li>Over <strong>170</strong> <a href="https://www.elsevier.com/about/open-science/open-access/open-access-journals">open access journals</a></li><li>Over <strong>1850</strong> hybrid journals</li></ul>
    </div>
    <div class="large-6 columns">
        <h3>Green Open access:</h3><p>After publication, a draft version of an article can be made publicly available after a time delay (also known as an embargo period). No fee is required to be paid by authors, as green open access is supported by the subscription model.&nbsp;&nbsp;Author manuscripts should display a <a href="https://www.elsevier.com/about/policies/sharing/how-to-attach-a-user-license">CC BY NC ND user license. </a></p><ul><li>All our journals provide an option for authors to self-archive</li><li><strong>110</strong> journals feature an <a href="https://www.elsevier.com/about/open-science/open-access/open-archive">open archive</a></li><li>We support CHORUS partners with <a href="https://www.elsevier.com/about/open-science/open-access/chorus">open manuscripts</a></li></ul>
    </div>
</div> </div><div class="row" style="padding-bottom:0;"><hr/>
<div class="large-12 columns" id="fact1_fullwidth_title_311099">
<h3 style="padding-bottom:20px;">Uptake of open access across the industry</h3>
</div>
</div><div class="row">
<div class="large-6 columns" id="fact1_lhs_uptake_311103">
<p>In 2017 there were an estimated <strong>2.2 million subscription</strong> and <strong>0.5 million open access</strong> articles published worldwide.</p><p>Open access now represents an estimated <strong>15% of the total amount of articles</strong> produced each year.</p><p>At Elsevier, we published 430,000 articles in 2017, <strong>over 27,000 of which were open access</strong>.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="fact2"><span class="elsorange">Fact 2:</span><br />Our APC prices are value for money</h2>

<div class="large-6 columns" id="fact2_lhs_text_311118">

        <h2><span class="elsorange"><a name="fact3"></a><a href="#fact3">Fact 3:</span><br/>We are making it easier to find and use OA content </a></h2>
        <p>Articles on ScienceDirect are tagged with "Open Access" and you can search, browse and set up alerts for open access journals.</p>
        <p>You can also search for open access articles within hybrid titles using the "contains open access" filter.</p>
        <p>All open access articles on ScienceDirect are also clearly labelled with a <a href="https://www.elsevier.com/about/policies/open-access-licenses/elsevier-user-license">user license</a> to help users understand how the article can be reused. </p>
        <p>Scopus indexes <strong>over 3,700</strong> open access journals from more than <strong>5,000</strong> publishers.</p>
        <p>It is now possible to search for open access on an article level within Scopus. </p>
        <p><a href="http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/jrnlallbooks/all-open-access" class="cta-primary" style="z-index: -1;" target="_blank">Search OA articles</a></p>

        <h2><span class="elsorange"><a name="fact5"></a><a href="#fact5">Fact 5:</span> We encourage authors to share their data </a></h2>
        <p>Elsevier is enabling researchers to store, share, discover and reuse data. We have:</strong></p>
        <ul>
            <li>55,000 users each month utilizing open data tools which include Mendeley Data and Hivebench</li>
            <li>1.6 million links from published articles to datasets stored in 65 external data repositories</li>
            <li>8.2 million datasets from over 30 repositories indexed and findable on Mendeley Data</li>
            <li>Help researchers to digitalize their data on Hivebench with 10,000 complete electronic notebooks created with 25,000 experiments recorded.</li>
        </ul>
        <p>Although the benefits of open research data are well known, in practice, confusion remains within the researcher community around when and how to share research data. A new <a href="https://www.elsevier.com/about/open-science/research-data/open-data-report">report from Elsevier and CWTS</a> reveals more about the challenges of open data.</p>
